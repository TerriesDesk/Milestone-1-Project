About Me



I am a librarian, writer, researcher, and web developer.

After 7 years as a producer for Public Television and 16 years as a public librarian, I embarked on a new adventure in self-employment. I now work as a freelance writer and web developer specializing in libraries and nonprofits. I am passionate about creating more clarity, understanding, and joy in this world. My strengths include clean web design, research and analysis, writing and communication, database management, cataloging and metadata.
